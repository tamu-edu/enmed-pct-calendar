*DISCLAIMER*
Google Calendar may take up to 24–48 hours to refresh subscribed ICS calendars. Refresh intervals are controlled by Google and are outside of TAMU’s control.

Schedule updates and urgent changes may also be communicated through Teams and/or email when appropriate.

This calendar is considered an official scheduling resource. Students are responsible for reviewing updates regularly and remaining aware of schedule changes.

To subscribe in Google Calendar:

Google Calendar → Add calendar → From URL

Copy the link for the calendar(s) you would like to subscribe to and paste it into the URL field.
